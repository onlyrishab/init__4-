📖 Sign Guide
20 gestures recognized by the camera · hold each sign steady for ~1 second
🖐️
STOP
Open palm facing the camera
  • Raise your hand to chest/face level
  • Spread all 5 fingers wide open
  • Face your palm directly toward the camera
  • Hold still for ~1 second
control · open palm
👍
HELP
Thumbs up — triggers emergency
  • Make a fist with your hand
  • Extend only your thumb upward
  • Keep all other fingers curled in
  • Hold steady — speaks immediately
intent · thumbs up
✊
YES
Fist + nod (vertical shake)
  • Close all fingers into a tight fist
  • Move your fist up and down (nodding motion)
  • Keep the motion steady, 2-3 nods
  • Fist alone = partial detection
intent · fist + vertical motion
✌️
NO
Peace sign + side-to-side shake
  • Extend index and middle fingers (peace/V sign)
  • Curl ring finger and pinky down
  • Shake your hand left-right (waving "no")
  • Keep fingers extended while shaking
intent · V-sign + lateral motion
☝️
HEAD
Point upward near top of frame
  • Extend only your index finger
  • Keep all other fingers curled
  • Point upward toward the top of the camera frame
  • Fingertip should be in the upper 40% of the screen
location · index up + high position
🤚
FEVER
Open palm held high (forehead level)
  • Open your hand with all fingers spread
  • Raise hand to forehead/face level
  • Wrist should be in the upper 45% of the frame
  • Like checking temperature on your forehead
symptom · open palm + high position
🫱
CHEST
Flat palm at chest height
  • Open your hand with fingers spread
  • Place palm at mid-chest level
  • Wrist between 30-65% height in the frame
  • Like placing hand over your heart area
location · open palm + mid position
✊
PAIN
Closed fist at chest/belly zone
  • Make a tight fist (all fingers curled)
  • Hold at chest or belly level (don't move)
  • Wrist between 30-75% height in the frame
  • Don't nod — that triggers YES instead
symptom · fist + mid position · stay still
🫳
STOMACH
Flat palm held low (belly level)
  • Open your hand with fingers spread
  • Hold palm low, near stomach/waist level
  • Wrist should be below 58% of the frame
  • Like resting hand on your belly
location · open palm + low position
🌀
NAUSEA
Hand low + circular motion
  • Place hand low near your stomach
  • Move your hand in slow circles
  • Make at least 1 full circular rotation
  • Keep the motion smooth and continuous
symptom · low position + circular motion
💧
WATER
W-sign held near the mouth
  • Extend index, middle, and ring fingers (W shape)
  • Curl thumb and pinky inward
  • Hold the sign near your chin/mouth
  • Hold steady for ~1 second
need · W-sign + high position
😷
COUGH
Fist held directly over the mouth
  • Make a tight fist
  • Place your fist directly in front of your mouth
  • Ensure the fist is centered horizontally
  • Hold steady
symptom · fist + mouth level
💫
DIZZY
Hand circling near the head
  • Raise your hand near the side of your head
  • Make slow, continuous circular motions
  • Ensure at least 1 full rotation
symptom · high position + circular motion
🗣️
THROAT
Index pointing horizontally across neck
  • Extend only your index finger
  • Point horizontally across your neck/throat area
  • Keep the hand centered and steady
location · index + neck level
👁️
EYE
Index finger near the eye
  • Extend only your index finger
  • Point very high, near your eye level
  • Hold steady
location · index + very high position
🙏
THANK YOU
Flat palm moving outward/downward
  • Start with an open palm near your chin
  • Move the hand smoothly outward and downward
  • Keep fingers fully extended
intent · open palm + downward motion
🩺
DOCTOR
V-sign held steady at chest level
  • Extend index and middle fingers (V shape)
  • Hold steady near your mid-chest
  • Do not shake (shaking NO uses V-sign laterally)
need · V-sign + mid position
⚡
SEVERE
Tight fist shaking rapidly side-to-side
  • Make a tight fist
  • Shake it rapidly left and right
  • Keep the motion quick and continuous
severity · fist + fast lateral motion
💊
MEDICINE
Pinky extended at chest level
  • Extend only your pinky finger (I sign)
  • Curl all other fingers and thumb
  • Hold steady at chest level
need · pinky only + mid position
🚽
BATHROOM
Y-sign shaking side-to-side
  • Extend thumb and pinky (Y sign)
  • Curl middle fingers
  • Shake your hand laterally (left-right)
need · Y-sign + lateral motion
